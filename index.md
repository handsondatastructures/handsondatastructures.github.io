**Hands On Data Structures** is a CS education tutorial designed for conference workshops and professional learning. Participants learn core data structures by building, moving, and transforming physical models before translating them back to code.

## At a glance

- **Audience:** CS educators and instructional leaders
- **Format:** Hands-on workshop with small-group facilitation
- **Duration:** 90 minutes (adaptable to 60 minutes, 2 hours, or half-day)
- **Focus:** Stacks, queues, linked lists, trees, graphs, and hashing
